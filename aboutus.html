Our Story

Authentic Taste Meets Rustic Charm

Little Village brings together Andhra village warmth and multi-cuisine dining in one relaxed, family-friendly space. The restaurant is designed to feel grounded and welcoming, with a setting that mirrors the quiet charm of rural life.

From everyday meals to celebrations, the experience is built around comfort, flavour, and generous hospitality. The function hall, open dining areas, and signature dishes are all part of that same village-inspired atmosphere.
